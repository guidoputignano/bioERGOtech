Event Overview

The bioERGOtech Innovation Summit marks a pivotal moment in the transformation of biotech research. This exclusive launch event brings together leading experts from academia, industry, and government to explore the revolutionary potential of AI-driven laboratory automation.

Our distinguished speakers will present cutting-edge research, innovative technologies, and strategic insights that are reshaping how we approach chronic disease treatments. From genetic analysis platforms to digital health solutions, this summit showcases the future of intelligent biotech research.
