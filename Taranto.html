The Taranto Story

The Taranto Hub is bioERGOtech's first innovation center, strategically established in a city with a rich industrial past and poised for a revolutionary future in healthcare and biotechnology.

For decades, Taranto has been known for its steel production and industrial might. However, this legacy has left the region with significant healthcare challenges and environmental concerns that demand innovative solutions.

Our hub represents a pivotal transformation in Taranto's story - leveraging the region's industrial expertise and engineering capabilities to pioneer advances in biological research automation and healthcare innovation.

Optimized for Local Challenges

The Taranto Hub's facilities are being specially configured to address the unique health challenges of industrial regions. Our equipment and methodologies will be optimized for studying the complex interactions between environmental factors and chronic disease development.

With dedicated laboratory spaces for environmental sample analysis, cellular response studies, and automated experimental systems, we're creating a comprehensive research ecosystem that will accelerate discoveries relevant to the local population.

  • Specialized environmental contaminant detection systems
  • Biomarker identification platforms for industrial exposure
  • High-throughput screening for therapeutic compound discovery
